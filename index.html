About Us

Muriel, with its brilliant and focused range of products, has redefined luxury spa. Muriel has created global skin care lines, with a continued commitment to innovation and quality. Muriel set out to change the landscape of skin care within the spa market through the creation of the Muriel Spa Collection. To maintain the highest levels of quality and standards in manufacturing, Muriel also opened an independent laboratory and R&D Centre.

Muriel is the brainchild of a team of entrepreneurs who have tremendous experience in the skin care and cosmetology and is renowned for their ability to pick up the pulse of the market.

Muriel skin care for face and body addresses all skin concerns. The quest for beauty is as old as the hills - and has always been inspired by plant life, Muriel skin care gives you an exotic skin care range. Muriel skin care is a combination of high tech ingredients and nourishing botanical extracts.

The Collection

Based on high concentrations of carefully selected botanical and plant extracts, recognized for their soothing and restorative powers, the Muriel Spa Collection marries cutting edge science with nature. Masylne's belief in both paraben and preservative free treatments combined with hydrosol technology, which distills active ingredients to its purest form, delivers unparalleled results.

Key aspects of the Muriel Collection are:

  • Muriel is a European formula manufactured in India
  • Muriel is Paraben free and suitable for all skin types
  • Muriel has been introduced only as single use kits and tailor made for individual Spas.
  • Muriel is all natural and can be combined with natural fruits and vegetables to create further customized solutions.
  • The Muriel line caters to the face, body, hands and feet.
  • Muriel has a 2 year shelf life after the packaging is open.
  • Each single use kits will have the complete set of products needed for a treatment.
